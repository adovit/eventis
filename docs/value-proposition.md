# Value Proposition — Eventis

**Last updated**: 2026-02-28

---

## One-liner
> Eventis is the trusted Lithuanian marketplace for buying and selling secondary tickets — safer than Facebook groups, more local than Viagogo.

---

## The Problem

### For Sellers (ticket holders who can't attend)
- **Facebook groups** are the default. Scams are common. No payment protection.
- Buyers ghost after agreeing to price. Sellers have no recourse.
- Sharing PDF tickets is risky — buyer can screenshot and share further.
- No verified identity = no trust.

### For Buyers (event-goers seeking tickets)
- Primary tickets sell out fast for popular events (Žalgiris, concerts).
- Facebook resellers charge arbitrary prices with no accountability.
- No guarantee ticket is valid / hasn't been sold multiple times.
- International platforms (Viagogo, StubHub) don't cover Lithuanian events well.

---

## The Solution — Eventis

| Need | How Eventis solves it |
|------|-----------------------|
| Sellers want to safely sell | Stripe-protected payment before PDF transfer |
| Buyers want ticket validity | Seller identity verified (SmartID, Phase 2) |
| Both want fair pricing | Transparent marketplace, seller sets price |
| Both want local coverage | Scraped from bilietai.lt, zalgiris.koobin.com, kakava.lt |
| Sellers want timely payout | Automated payout tracking, paid after event |

---

## Value Proposition Canvas

### Customer Jobs
- **Seller**: Get money back for tickets they can't use
- **Buyer**: Get into a sold-out event they care about
- **Admin**: Operate a marketplace that makes money and stays trusted

### Pains
- Seller: Fear of scam, no-show buyer, unsafe payment
- Buyer: Fear of fake tickets, overpaying, no customer support
- Market: Facebook is broken for this; international platforms ignore LT

### Gains
- Seller: Guaranteed payment via Stripe, identity badge = more buyer trust
- Buyer: Verified seller, PDF ticket delivery, order history
- Market: First mover in structured LT secondary ticket market

### Products & Services
- Event discovery (scraped, always fresh)
- Seller listing (PDF upload, price, seat info)
- Stripe-secured purchase with PDF delivery
- Seller payout tracking
- SmartID identity verification (roadmap)

### Pain Relievers
- Stripe holds funds until delivery confirmed
- PDF delivered automatically — no need to trust a stranger
- SmartID verifies seller identity (roadmap)
- Admin can intervene in disputes

### Gain Creators
- Lithuania-specific events (not on international platforms)
- Lithuanian UI (no language barrier)
- Seller earns money, buyer gets ticket — everyone wins

## Target Market

### Primary (MVP)
- **Sellers**: 18–40, urban, Lithuanian, concert/sports event regulars
- **Buyers**: 18–40, urban, Lithuanian, last-minute buyers

### Secondary (Growth)
- Corporate buyers (bulk tickets for teams)
- International expats seeking Lithuanian events
